How a local family turned a neighborhood gas station into the heart of the community.

The Beginning

Doors Opened, Roots Planted

Back in 2025, our family took a leap of faith. We didn't just want to build a business; we wanted to build a place where neighbors could catch up, grab a fresh cup of coffee, and get safely on their way. When we first flipped the switch on the Citigo sign, the neighborhood looked a bit different, but our vision remained exactly the same.

As a independent, family-owned business, we’ve poured our hearts into this corner. Over the decades, we've watched kids who used to come in for after-school candy grow up and bring their own children in for road trip snacks.

Our Two Simple Rules:

"Keep the fuel dependable, the coffee scorching hot, and never let a neighbor leave without a smile."

— The Founder

Rooted in the Community

We don't just work here; we live here. Supporting local youth sports, neighborhood clean-ups, and regional food drives isn't a corporate mandate for us—it's just what neighbors do for neighbors. When you buy from our food mart, that support goes right back into funding local school programs and community spaces.

A True Customer-First Approach

We know you have choices on where to fill up your tank or grab a quick bite. That’s why we focus on the details that matter: sparkling clean restrooms, well-lit pumps, fresh food options, and an actual human greeting you when you step through the door. If something isn't right, let us know—we'll make it right on the spot.

Stop In and Say Hello

Whether you need a full tank of high-quality Citgo fuel for a long haul, a quick morning breakfast sandwich, or just a friendly face on a stressful day, our doors are open. Come on in—we’d love to meet you.
